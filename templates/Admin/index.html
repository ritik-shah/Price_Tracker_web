Price Tracker App

Pricesearch enables you to automatically track your competition. You’ll receive updates regarding the prices of your competitors’ products.

Price Tracker is a tool to track, compare, and analysis prices and is a technical solution that helps (online) retailors, wholesalers, and manufactures track price of competitors and dealers. It's no secret that marketplaces are the winner's of today's eCommerce playing field. Amazon, Idealo, Flipkart, etc. are good examples of marketplaces that offer retailers a platform to sell their stuff. Price Tracker makes the process easier & less painfull, and it gives information to make pricing decisions. By having the right prices, you incerase the chance of selling time. It's a misconception that the primary purpose of a price tracker is to lower your prices. That means upping your prices, thus increasing your margins, if the opportunities are there...!
